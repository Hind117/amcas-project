About

Arab Mars Club for Astronomy and Space

Is an initiative by ambitious young boys and girls form all around the Arabic world to spread the knowledge of astronomy and astrophysics, where we work hard to leave a notable influence in the growth of this field in Saudi Arabia all to the Arabic gulf and the Arabic world as whole.

AMCAS club's objectives and highlights:



- Spreading awareness to the astrology and all its subcategories.



- Preparing informative events that specialize in cosmology science around the arabic world with the organization and participation of the club's members.



- Generating a distinctive footprint that takes part in the upcoming development in Saudi arabia regarding cosmology science as well as other arabic countries.



AMCAS club's objectives and highlights:



- Improving or correcting the common misinterpretation of facts with unproven theories revolving around cosmology matter.



- Aims toward the continuous improvement of: personal skills, special experience, and innovation of Amcas club's members.



- To the amcas club achieving its goals for the development of the Scientific informations in general.
